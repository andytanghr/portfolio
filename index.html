About Me

I am a full stack web developer living in Houston, TX. I attended the University of Texas at Austin for my undergraduate degrees in chemistry and Latin, and later for my master's degree in historical musicology.

I have traveled the world extensively and worked in Hong Kong and El Salvador. I have been a project manager, teacher, chemist, musician/musicologist, philologist/Latinist, and art dealer.

Computing and technology have always been a part of my life. Since attending DigitalCrafts's 16-week full stack immersive program, I now make them part of my work. This website primarily showcases my programming projects.

I am also heavily involved in the art and music world with my art education nonprofit Folding Table Productions, and in community service after natural disasters, Relief Exchange.
