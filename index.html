I am a full stack web developer living in Houston, TX. I attended the University of Texas at Austin for my undergraduate degrees in chemistry and Latin, and later for my master's degree in historical musicology. I have traveled the world extensively and worked in Hong Kong and El Salvador. I have worked as a project manager, teacher, chemist, musician/musicologist, philologist/Latinist, and art dealer.

Computing and technology has always been a part of my life. After attending DigitalCrafts's four-month full stack immersive program I now make them part of my work. This website primarily discusses my programming projects, but I also link to my other projects in art education and curating, Folding Table Productions, and in community service after natural disasters, Relief Exchange.
