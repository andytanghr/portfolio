About Me

I am a software engineer and a full stack web developer living in Houston, TX. I attended the University of Texas at Austin for my undergraduate degrees in chemistry and Latin, and later for my master's degree in historical musicology.

I have traveled the world extensively and worked in Hong Kong and El Salvador. I have been a project manager, teacher, chemist, musician/musicologist, philologist/Latinist, and art curator.

Computing and technology have always been a part of my life. Since attending DigitalCrafts's 16-week full stack immersive program, I now make them part of my work. This website primarily showcases my tech projects.

I am also involved in the art and music world with my art education nonprofit Folding Table Productions, and in community service after natural disasters with my other nonprofit Relief Exchange.

Bidder

repo

Bidder is a real-time art auction web app that takes customers from gallery display to final payment. It supports multiple live and future auctions; user accounts to place bids, get email updates, and pay for winning bids. Bidding is recorded in real-time, and credit and debit cards are accepted payment options.
